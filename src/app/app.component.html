Material-Design-Ng2-Floatings

Standalone Angular directive for Material floating action buttons. Shamelessly Openly inspired by Google Inbox, ng-material-floating-button Evernote and Path.

It works out of the box with no other dependencies apart from Angular, but plays nicely with Angular Material.
